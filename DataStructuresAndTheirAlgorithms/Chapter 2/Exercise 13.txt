Q.

Show that the sum on page 58 has the value 2^(k+1) - k - 2.

A.

  2^(k-1)*1 + 2^(k-2)*2 + ... + 1*k
= 2^(k-1)*1 + 2^(k-2)*1 + ... + 1*1
+             2^(k-2)*1 + ... + 1*1
                  .
		  .
		  .
+                               1*1
= 2^k + 2^(k-1) + ... + 2^1 + 2^0 - k - 1
= 2^(k+1) - 1 - k - 1
= 2^(k+1) - k - 2
